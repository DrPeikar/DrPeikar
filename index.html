Dr. Mehdi Peikar, is an orthodontist and a physicist and a graduate of the UCLA School of Dentistry, is the inventor and founder of BRIUS. He also holds a Ph.D. in Biomechanics from Johns Hopkins University and a Master's degree in Quantum Mechanics and Condensed Matter from the University of Illinois. Utilizing his experience in biomechanics, Dr. Peikar engineered the BRIUS system, known also as Independent Mover or Brava, harnessing the latest in 3D printing, material science, and AI to deliver superior biomechanics for tooth movement. Following braces and aligners, BRIUS represents the third evolution in teeth movement techniques.

Dr. Peikar has secured over 60 patents and BRIUS Technologies Inc., the company he founded has raised more than $50 million in funding so far.

Brava By Brius

Dr. Mehdi Peikar, a UCLA-trained orthodontist with extensive expertise in quantum mechanics, condensed matter, and biomechanics, is the inventor and founder of Brius Technologies. The flagship product, Brava, represents a groundbreaking advancement in orthodontic treatment, leveraging over 60 patents to offer a novel approach to teeth straightening. Unlike traditional methods, Brava introduces a unique system known as Independent Mover technology, which utilizes AI to plan and execute the independent and simultaneous movement of teeth, ensuring a customized, efficient, and patient-centric treatment experience. This innovation not only accelerates the orthodontic process but also enhances comfort and outcomes for patients. Brius Technologies has successfully raised over $50 million in funding, treated thousands of patients across the US and Asia, and solidified his reputation as a leading innovator in the field of orthodontics, poised to impact the future of dental care with his contributions.
